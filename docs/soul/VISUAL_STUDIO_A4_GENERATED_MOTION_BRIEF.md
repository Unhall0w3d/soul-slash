# Visual Studio A4: reviewed generated motion

## Objective

Promote the qualified Wan 2.2 TI2V Vulkan pilot into the smallest complete Visual Studio workflow: a reviewed still may seed one bounded motion study, the Operator reviews that immutable result, and an accepted result may be bound to one exact Music Studio candidate for full-duration rendering.

## Approved flow

1. The source must be an immutable Visual Studio still with a current `keep` review.
2. The Operator supplies a bounded motion direction and seed.
3. Preview binds the source digest, prompt, seed, fixed model profile, frame geometry, and output duration.
4. `GENERATE_VISUAL_MOTION` authorizes one foreground Wan invocation. The process terminates as complete, failed, canceled, awaiting input, or blocked for review.
5. The resulting four-second WebM, receipt, and bounded log are retained privately under the visual project.
6. The Operator records `keep` or `revise`. Only `keep` may advance.
   Any motion study may be permanently removed through a separate exact destructive preview/execute gate.
7. `BIND_VISUAL_COMPANION` copies the exact reviewed clip into one exact Music candidate lineage.
8. Music Studio previews and authorizes a CPU-only full-duration MP4 render. FFmpeg repeats the reviewed clip, scales/pads it, and muxes the exact candidate audio. It performs no new model inference.

## Boundaries

- Image-guided motion remains this slice's scope. Text-only video is implemented separately by the A5 native-video lane.
- Fixed 832×480, 33-frame, 8 fps profile using Wan 2.2 TI2V 5B Q4_K_M and TAE.
- No daemon, model server, watcher, queue, scheduled work, automatic retry, upload, or publication.
- Navigation may detach the browser from an accepted request, but no new persistent worker is introduced. The request-scoped bounded command finishes or is terminated by its timeout.
- Still-image companions remain supported and unchanged.
- Motion review and Music binding are separate human gates.

## Acceptance

- Deterministic fixtures prove source-review enforcement, exact digest gating, one bounded renderer invocation, immutable evidence, review gating, authenticated media delivery, exact Music binding, and full-duration loop/mux behavior.
- Existing Visual Studio A2 and Music visual companion verifiers remain green after their superseded “motion unavailable” assertions are updated.
- The real dashboard reports the pinned motion resources as ready on AMD-Free Core or Music Core.
